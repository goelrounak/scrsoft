MAINTENANCE SOFTWARE

SCR-MM is a cost effective and user friendly Maintenance management software application for organizations of all sizes. It includes Preventive Maintenance, Breakdown Maintenance and Predictive Maintenance, MTTR, MTBR.

SCR-MM helps companies avoid unplanned shutdowns and enables them to optimize Maintenance resources by creating automatic Maintenance schedules and allows them in preventing Breakdowns.

SCRQMS 4.0 suite is smart QMS (Quality Management System)and integrated Solution for smart industry which includes modules like: APQP, PPAP, SPC, Gauge Calibration and MSA, Supplier/Vendor Quality, Cost of Quality, Customer Complaints, Document control, Audit Managemrnt, PMS, Maintenance etc.
